Zimo the sensational pizza maker. He works all day to serve the best pizza in town and has dedicated his life to his craft. His pizza is so delicious word got out to the underworld and was offered eternal life from their council under one condition; He works in the underworld during the night. Zimo got the bargain of a lifetime, literally, he instantly accepted with glee! Now Zimo can live his dream of serving pizza for eternity.
